Peru's native communities hotspots

14ers in Chaffee County, CO, USA

Chaffee County in Colorado has 12 14ers, all shown in the map, we can see the closeness to different towns, and by adding trails, we can see which 14ers are privately owned.

Canadian Lynx habitat in Summit County, CO, USA

Lynx has a wide habitat in the county of Summit, however, due to the closeness to towns and ski resorts, there is a high chance of road kill which would decrease with the construction of a wildlife bridge.
